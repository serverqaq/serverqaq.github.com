Recite Poems in the Moonlight

Traditional Chinese Painting

Painter · Calligrapher · Member, China Artists Association

Ewen Wang (王志), known by his pen name Danyi Wang (号王丹一), was born in 1957 in Bozhou City, Anhui Province. He graduated from the Institute of Ancient Books of Jilin University and is dean of Jingzhong Academy of Painting and Calligraphy. His work spans oil painting, traditional Chinese ink painting, printmaking, Iwasai mineral painting, sketch, and calligraphy.
