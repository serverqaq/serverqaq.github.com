I am Danyi Wang

Zhi Wang, also known as Danyi Wang or Ye Wang, was born in 1957 in Bozhou City, Anhui Province. Zhi graduated from the Institute of Ancient Books of Jilin University. Zhi is a member of the China Artists Association, and dean of Jingzhong Academy of Painting and Calligraphy.
